How live shopping works?

We provide the fun and engagement necessary to attract new customers and boost your sales

1 Go live

Start your live selling broadcast on your website using our EFON widget. Create a product catalogue in our admin portal and display product cards to your customers during the stream.

2 Comment and Click

During your live stream, shoppers can simply click on a product to add it to their cart without leaving the stream

3 Checkout

Customers receive an instant checkout notification in their product cards once the stream is over
